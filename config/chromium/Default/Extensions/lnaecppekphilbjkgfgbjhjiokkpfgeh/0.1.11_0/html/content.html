Quick tutorial on how to use 'Read-It!'

  1. Click the link blow to open the try out site: https://www.nasa.gov/image-feature/cloudy-earth
    When the page is ready, right click on the Nasa web page, select "TTS Read-It! - Article to Speech" => "Read the article.."
  2. Now click here to try on an article page of the popular news site: https://www.nasa.gov/image-feature/cloudy-earth
    'Read-It!' requires your permission to validate your license, please grant OAuth2 to 'Read-It!'
  3. Click on "Reading" tab, for controling reading playback
  4. Click on "Settings" tab, for controlling the reading options
  5. Click on "Sites" tab, for creating or modifying the site templates
